TSUNAMI FESTIVAL: ART & MUSIC IN THE HEART OF COPENHAGEN

TSUNAMI Festival is a spectacular 3-day music, arts and technology festival situated in the heart of Copenhagen. The festival seeks out and presents the best talents from a wide range of creative subcultures within music, art, technology and design.

Tsunami offers more than 30 live concerts, a stage dedicated to kick ass DJ’s, and a festival area unlike anywhere else and this summer it’s our 10 year anniversary! We still have early birds tickets up for sale so grab yours before they are gone
